What is a 360° Virtual Tour?
A 360° virtual tour is an immersive experience that allows viewers to explore a location as if they were physically present. It uses 360° photographs stitched together to create a walkthrough that can be navigated online or on Google Maps.
How much does a 360° virtual tour cost?
Pricing depends on the size of your space and the number of rooms/areas to be covered. Contact us for a customized quote. We offer competitive packages for all business types.
How long does it take to deliver the tour?
Typically 3-5 working days from the shoot date. The tour will be published on Google Maps and you'll receive an embeddable link for your website.
Will the tour appear on Google Maps & Search?
Yes! Your virtual tour will be published directly on Google Street View and linked to your Google Business Profile. It appears in Google Maps, Google Search & Google Images.
Which areas do you cover for shoots?
We are based in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh and cover all areas of Kanpur including Kalyanpur, Kidwai Nagar, Civil Lines, and more. We also travel to Lucknow, Prayagraj, Varanasi, Agra, Delhi, and across India for shoots.
