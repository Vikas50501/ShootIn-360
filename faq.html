1 What is a 360° Virtual Tour? General
A 360° virtual tour is an immersive, interactive digital experience that allows viewers to explore a physical location as if they were actually present there. Using a professional 360° camera, we photograph every angle of your space. These images are then stitched together and published as a navigable tour — accessible on Google Maps, Google Street View, your website or any social media platform. Visitors can look in all directions, navigate between rooms and explore your business fully online before deciding to visit in person.
2 How much does a virtual tour cost in Kanpur? Pricing
Our pricing is customized based on the size of your space, the number of rooms or areas to be covered, the type of business and specific requirements such as hotspots or embed links. We offer competitive packages starting from small businesses and clinics all the way to large hotels and real estate developments. Contact us or WhatsApp us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your property.
3 How long does it take to deliver the virtual tour? General
Typically, your completed virtual tour is delivered within 3 to 5 working days from the date of the shoot. This includes all editing, stitching, hotspot navigation setup and Google Street View publishing. For urgent requirements, we do offer an express delivery option — please mention it at the time of booking and we will do our best to accommodate your timeline.
4 Will the virtual tour appear on Google Maps? Technical
Yes — absolutely. Your 360° virtual tour will be published directly on Google Street View and linked to your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business). This means your tour becomes visible in Google Maps, Google Search and Google Images when anyone searches for your business. Having a virtual tour on Google Maps significantly improves your listing's visibility and trustworthiness.

7 Is the virtual tour mobile friendly? Technical
Absolutely. All our virtual tours are fully responsive and work seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, laptops and desktop computers. Customers can explore your business using their mobile browser or through the Google Maps app without needing to install any additional software. The gyroscope sensor on smartphones also enables a natural head-turning navigation experience for an even more immersive feel.
8 Can I embed the virtual tour on my website? Technical
Yes — we provide you with an embed code that you can paste directly into your website's HTML. The tour will appear as an interactive iframe that visitors can explore without leaving your site. This is especially effective for hotels, real estate developers, schools and hospitals who want to keep potential customers engaged on their website for longer. We also provide a shareable link for social media and WhatsApp sharing.

We work with a wide range of businesses across sectors. If you have a physical space and want to showcase it online, we can create a tour for you.
11 Do you offer revisions after delivery? Booking
Yes — we offer one round of revisions after delivery at no additional charge. If there are any corrections needed in the tour such as adjusting navigation points, removing unwanted elements or tweaking the starting view, simply let us know within 7 days of delivery. Major re-shoots due to changes in the physical space may require a revisit fee, but we always discuss this transparently before proceeding.
12 How many rooms or areas can be covered in one tour? Booking
There is no fixed limit on the number of rooms or areas that can be included in your tour. Our packages are typically structured around the number of 360° viewpoints (called "nodes") in the tour. A small café might need 5–8 nodes; a large hotel might need 20–40 nodes. The more areas you include, the more comprehensive your tour becomes. We recommend covering all key customer-facing spaces to maximize the impact of your virtual tour.
13 What is Google Street View and how is it related? Technical
Google Street View is Google's platform for hosting and displaying 360° immersive imagery. Originally created for outdoor road-level photography, it now supports indoor business tours. As a Google Street View Trusted partner, Shootin 360 can upload your 360° business tour directly to Google Street View, where it becomes linked to your Google Business Profile. This means anyone who finds your business on Google Maps can instantly explore your interior with a single click — a powerful trust-building feature.
14 How does a virtual tour help my SEO? Technical
A 360° virtual tour on Google Maps significantly boosts your local SEO in several ways. Google rewards businesses that provide rich, engaging content on their listings — including virtual tours. Businesses with tours get up to 2x more engagement from Google users. Higher engagement signals to Google that your listing is relevant and active, which improves your ranking in local search results. Additionally, embedding the tour on your website increases average session duration, which further positively impacts your overall SEO performance.
15 Do I need to prepare anything before the shoot? Booking
Minimal preparation is needed, but a few simple things help make the shoot go smoothly. Ensure the space is clean, well-lit and clutter-free. Remove any unnecessary items from countertops, tables or floors. Switch on all lights including decorative and ambient lighting for the best results. Inform your staff about the shoot date so the area is not disrupted. For restaurants and hotels, make sure tables are set and the ambience reflects your best presentation. We will guide you through a checklist during the booking confirmation process.
